About Us

Galaxy Envitech (GE) provides professional, multidisciplinary services to support all aspects of Environment, mine development, from exploration and feasibility through to design, contract negotiations, construction supervision, mine management, operational assistance and training. GE also rendering consultancy services for Geotechnical Investigations, Topographical Survey, Planning and Designs, Supervision, Quality Monitoring and Preparing detailed Project Reports (DPR).

Our clients include a wide range of environment, mining and exploration companies; as well as lenders and investors.

GE staff are proficient in virtually the entire spectrum of mined commodities and are experienced in working all over the world.
